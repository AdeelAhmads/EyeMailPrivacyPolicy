Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when using EyeMail Screen Recorder.

Last updated: August 12, 2025

Information We Collect

EyeMail Screen Recorder is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our core functionality:

Important Note

We do NOT collect or access any personal emails, email content, or other sensitive information from your Gmail or Outlook accounts. Our extension only interacts with these platforms to facilitate video sharing.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve our video recording and sharing service:

  • Video Storage: Temporarily store your videos on our secure servers for sharing and download
  • Service Delivery: Enable video recording, editing, and sharing functionality
  • Technical Support: Diagnose and resolve technical issues you may encounter
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience (data is anonymized)
  • Communication: Send important updates about the service or respond to your inquiries

Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Limited sharing occurs only in these specific circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Trusted cloud storage providers for secure video hosting
  • Analytics services for anonymized usage statistics
  • Technical support providers for customer service

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

Your Control

You maintain full control over your videos. You can delete your recordings at any time, and we will remove them from our servers within 30 days.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Videos are stored on encrypted, secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can view your data
  • Regular Audits: Regular security assessments and updates
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information and delete it when no longer needed

Data Retention

We retain your information for the minimum time necessary:

  • Video Content: Stored until you delete it, or automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity
  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained for up to 2 years for service improvement
  • Technical Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days

Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Cookies and Tracking

Our extension uses minimal local storage for functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality and user preferences
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our extension (anonymized)
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies

You can manage these settings through your browser's extension settings.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes through:

  • Extension notifications
  • Email notifications (if you've provided an email address)
  • Updates on our website

Continued use of our extension after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, we're here to help.
